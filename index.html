Invoke Often!

Class D Libri - Instructions & Rituals

Second Edition

Do what thou wilt shall be the whole of the Law.

Welcome to the homepage of the book Invoke Often!, a collection of mostly 'Class D' rituals and instructions as informed by the syllabus in the Equinox Vol. 1, No. 10, with some additional selected material.

Includes Libers E vel Exercitiorum, Jugorum, Librae, RV vel Spiritus, Batrachophrenoboocosmomachia, O vel Manus et Sagittae, B vel Magi, Os Abysmi vel Daath, Israfel, Thisharb, A vel Armorum, Resh vel Helios, V vel Reguli, HHH, Iod, Turris vel Domus Dei, Had, Nu, Astarte vel Berylli, Samekh, Os Abysmi vel Daath, the Vision of the Eighth Aethyr, the Star Ruby, the Star Sapphire, the Mass of the Phoenix, One Star in Sight, the instructions for invoking Aethyrs from the 18th Aethyr, and Graduum Montis Abiegni. The appendix contains a short reference for Greek and Hebrew characters and their values, 'the Holy Hexagram' as a supplement for the Star Sapphire, and the adoration from Liber L as a supplement for Liber Resh.
