Invoke Often!

Instructions, Rituals, and the Great Work

Do what thou wilt shall be the whole of the Law.

Welcome to the homepage of the book Invoke Often!, a collection of mostly 'Class D' rituals and instructions as informed by the syllabus in the Equinox Vol. 1, No. 10, with some additional selected material.

Includes Libers E vel Exercitiorum, Jugorum, RV vel Spiritus, Batrachophrenoboocosmomachia, O vel Manus et Sagittae [Abridged -- missing the Greater rituals of the Pentagram and Hexagram], Thisharb, A vel Armorum, Resh vel Helios, V vel Reguli, HHH, Iod, Turris vel Domus Dei, Had, Nu, Astarte vel Berylli, Samekh, Os Abysmi vel Daath, the Vision of the Eighth Aethyr, the Star Ruby, the Star Sapphire, the Mass of the Phoenix, an abridged One Star in Sight, and Graduum Montis Abiegni. The appendix contains a short reference for elemental pentagrams and hexagrams, Liber V vel Reguli averse pentagrams, Greek and Hebrew characters and their values, as well as 'the Holy Hexagram' as a supplement for the Star Sapphire.
